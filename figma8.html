30 Min Meditation Challenge
"Follow these simple steps to guide you through a relaxing meditation experience."
Breathing (5 min) - Focus on your breath. Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, and exhale for 6 seconds.
Mindfulness (10 min) - Focus on your breath or a single point. If your mind wanders, gently bring it back.
Body Scan (10 min) - Starting from your toes, move up through your body, relaxing each muscle group.
Gratitude (5 min) - Reflect on something you are grateful for, sending love to yourself and others.
